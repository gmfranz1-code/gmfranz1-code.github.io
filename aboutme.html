About Me

Hi! My name is Gianna Franz and I am a student at Arizona State University. I created this website because I wanted to change the struggles college students face with meal planning and eating healthy. I created a space where all of your favorite meals can be accessible and where you can create a meal calendar to stay organized. I hope you enjoy using MealTime as much as I enjoyed creating it!
